About

Everest Media Group

Everest media group was founded with the purpose of providing a central location for musicians to acquire media services. Our team of music producers, sound engineers, graphic designers and web designers work creatively to provide musicians with quality art to help them build a professional image. With a catalog of dozens of high quality instrumentals for sale, our beat store is an industry leader to buy hip-hop beats from. Each of our producers specialize in their own sound. Collectively, EverestMG provides the best beats of all genres from hip hop beats, Rnb beats, smooth beats to sampled and oldschool beats.

Mission

Our mission is to provide artists with the essentials to form a professional image. Through our team, we are able to help artists form branding and be able to market themselves to compete with industry professionals. By focusing on independent artists, we are able to provide them with affordable high quality works. We value each of our customers and are determined to help them reach their potential, therefore, we offer support throughout the whole process from choosing beats, to creating graphic art for mixtape covers.

Areas of Practice

With a team of media experts, we are able to cater to any media needs. We specialize in music production, graphic design, website design. After recently partnering with Scratchdown Records / FameCity Gang engineer Julio Alfonso we now also offer mixing and mastering services. With a professional studio, the following equipment is used to get our professional sound: mastering Pro tools 10, Logic pro X, Universal audio 1176 Distressor, Avalon vacuum tube , API 55oA, Waves plug-ins , UDA plug-ins , Slate digital plug-ins.
